# Pump Demo Images

This directory should contain 6 product photos of a Positive Displacement Pump (Waukesha / SPX Flow, PN: U1 015 / 015-U1).

## Required Files

- `pump-1.jpg` - Main hero image: Full pump photo on clean background
- `pump-2.jpg` - Close-up of pump housing and connections
- `pump-3.jpg` - Side view showing pump body and mounting
- `pump-4.jpg` - Nameplate/identification label (PN: U1 015) close-up
- `pump-5.jpg` - Top view showing pump head and seals
- `pump-6.jpg` - Detail view of pump internals/rotors

## Image Requirements

- Product-style photos (no people, no office/engineering desk shots)
- Realistic industrial equipment photos
- Different angles and views of the same pump type
- High quality, suitable for product display

## Usage

These images are referenced in `src/components/RepairDetailsDrawer.tsx` in the `getAttachments` function.
